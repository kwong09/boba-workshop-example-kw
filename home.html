About CAMS Friendship Club

CAMS Friendship Club is a chapter of The Friendship Foundation. Our goal is to make a difference in the lives of youth with special needs! In order to follow through with our goal, we plan on hosting events to connect students from CAMS with younger kids to make lasting friendships.
